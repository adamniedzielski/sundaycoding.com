Why do I need a code quality report?

People don't discuss the clean code practices for the sake of esthetics. The code is written to deliver the business value. Low quality code will slow down the future growth. It negatively impacts the ability to implement new features. It increases the cost of maintaining the software. Bad code costs you a lot of money!

On the other hand, it's really hard to think about the wider perspective when the new features are what matters most NOW. Fortunately, there are external experts who can do it for you.

Codebase Health Check is your unique chance to get a second opinion on the quality of your code. It's a fresh look from the person who wasn't involved in writing the code. The report allows you to estimate the state of code quality in your project. It contains practical tips and suggestion what should be improved.

What will be covered?
